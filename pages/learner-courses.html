Mathematical Methods I provides a rigorous foundation in the mathematical techniques essential to actuarial science. Topics include differential and integral calculus, ordinary differential equations, linear algebra, and numerical methods. Students will develop both theoretical understanding and practical problem-solving skills applied throughout actuarial models.
